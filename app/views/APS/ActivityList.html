II. Brief Context/Perspective of the Activity

Being part of the La Salle Computer Society, our main goal is to guide our fellow college mates and offer our best service to them. We help the students reach out to the corporate world by opening up avenues for opportunities for them to reach their full potential through the programs and activities that we provide. The problem we usually encounter is that students are too concentrated on their academic that they fail to maximize their stay in De La Salle University.

The solution that we came up is the overnight activity called No Tech camp. Through this project, students may build relationship with the college and have a taste of something out of their normal life. We feel that in order for us to provide our college mates a better service, we must be able to provide activities that help them develop skills that cannot be taught inside a regular classroom set-up.

We came up with this project because we thought that this would be an effective way to bond with fellow Lasallians. We are sure that this event will allow Computer Studies students to bond and meet new people. In that, we will commit a lot of time and effort to make this project successful and as effective as possible.
