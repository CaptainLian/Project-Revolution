I. Objectives

  • To generate funds for the expenses of the No Tech Camp
  • To raise funds for the activities of the organization
  • To advertise the No Tech Camp event

II. Brief Context

Being part of the La Salle Computer Society, our main goal is to guide our fellow college mates and offer our best service to them. We help the students reach out to the corporate world by opening up avenues for opportunities for them to reach their full potential through the programs and activities that we provide. The problem we usually encounter is the limited funds for the organization that limits us in creating bigger and better events for the college. The funds that will be collected from this project will be used for future activities and projects that will better serve the students.

The solution that we came up with is the ticket selling for the No Tech camp. Through this project, we can generate funds that will support our projects in order for our organization to reach our main goal. We feel that in order for us to provide our college mates better service, we must have sufficient funds that will support us all throughout the new school year.

We came up with this project because we thought that this would be an effective way to raise our funds. We are sure that this event will allow Computer Studies students to bond and meet new people. In that, we will commit a lot of time and effort to make this project successful and as effective as possible.
